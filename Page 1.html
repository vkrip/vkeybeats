    Vkeybeats best known as Vk drip is a young, enthusiastic, and hardworking music producer based in Kenya. He is known for his unique music creation with the top-notch production and that’s what sets him aside from other producers.


    During a recent interview by our great team with Palmer Omosh’s now producer, Vkeybeats was quoted saying, “Music venture requires passion, consistency, and creativity . I didn’t just wake up to become the big producer I now am, it took me time to build and nurture my brand” In his two years plus experience in beat making and production he shares positive insights on his musical journey having worked with Big Boy T, Price no Bei, Mista Lee, Black King, and now Palmer Omosh . He is set to bring more surprises and hits to the music industry.



    Vkeybeats and Palmer Omosh

    His two latest creations Sitaki story zako & Twende Home by Palmer Omosh took the showbiz industry by storm, giving him the name and outreach platform as Vk drip 4 life. The top-ranked producer has now set standards and in no time he is soon to attain the intentional level.
